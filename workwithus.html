Work With Us

If you would like to engage with talented STEM students who are interested in using data
to add value to your company, consider sponsoring ICDSS, the largest data science focused
society in the UK!

Sponsoring ICDSS

At ICDSS, we highly value our industrial partnerships and the contribution they bring to our members. We invite organisations involved in all industries to join us and engage with our community to train the next generation of top data scientists.

Recruitment

Consistently ranked amongst one of the top universities in the world, our members are amongst the brightest in their fields. We offer our industry partners the opportunity to engage with our members and access their profiles and CVs.

Marketing

With a reach of almost 3,000 students from various backgrounds at Imperial College London, we can provide sponsors with access to our mailing list, provide brand visibility on our media, fixed advertisements on our website and weekly emails, opportunities for presentations to students and more.

AI Hack

Our flagship event of the year, AI Hack, invites over 350 universiy students from Imperial as well as other universities to take part in an overnight Data Hackathon. Sponsors of this event can enjoy their logo on the event, have recruitment booths, and even suggest datasets and later judge submissions.

Sponsorship packages 📦

There are a number of ways your company can sponsor our society. We have tailored our sponsorship packages to suit your needs and make the most of supporting a society from one of the world’s leading universities.

Please send us an email to express interest and our Head of Corporate Relations will be in touch!

Education partnership 🤝

In the upcoming academic year we aim to expand our workshop series by inviting relevant companies to teach essential data science skills. Your company can get involved in the following ways:
- Give training workshops targeting members with various technical backgrounds
- Bring insights into how data science brings value to your business by presenting real-world projects and applications of data-centric solutions

Opportunities💡

If there are any opportunites you wish to explore, standard requests such as an internship advertisement or wild ideas such as a joint-society conference, a demo day or other exciting plans, pop us an email and we'll follow up!
