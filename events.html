Our Events

Our society is dedicated to connecting Imperial's broad student base with industry, allowing our members to gain exclusive knowledge of how data science is applied in the field.

We also host talks from our very own Data Science Institute, where academics introduce their cutting edge research and futuristic insights. These events attract a wide variety of individuals from our large and diverse member base, including masters, final-year students and undergraduates getting a first taste of data science.

Upcoming Events
